This graph describes the change in human activity in the 80 days. The vertical bars represents the rate of change in numbers and the horizontal lines counts the days from starting from 20.04.2020. The y axis values are at times negative as the number of people participating declined. This shows how rapidly people adapted the self isolation method to combat a pandemic like COVID19. We find out that as we approach April, the grocery shops open up as it can bring great threats to the economy otherwise.
